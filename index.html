Research & Projects

Econometric Modeling of Financial Market Volatility

Technologies: MATLAB, Time-Series Analysis, GARCH Models

This project focused on developing and backtesting advanced econometric models to forecast asset volatility. It leveraged conditional heteroskedasticity frameworks (GARCH) to capture the nuances of market dynamics and provide more accurate risk assessments.

Algorithmic Corporate Risk Assessment Protocol

Technologies: Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn), Financial Statement Analysis, VBA

Designed and implemented an automated system to analyze corporate financial statements and market data. The protocol identifies key risk indicators through quantitative metrics, generating objective and scalable risk profiles for A-share companies.

Alpha Factor Identification via Machine Learning

Technologies: R, Factor Analysis, Supervised & Unsupervised Learning

Applied a suite of machine learning techniques to a large dataset of historical market data. The research aimed to identify and validate novel alpha-generating factors, moving beyond traditional linear models to uncover more complex patterns.
